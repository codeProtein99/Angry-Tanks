Game Featues

PLACE

The PLACE command will place the toy drone on the surface at position X,Y and facing NORTH, SOUTH, EAST, or WEST. The origin (0,0) is the SOUTHWEST most corner.

Note: The first valid command to the drone is a PLACE command. After the PLACE command, any sequence of commands may be issued, including another PLACE command. All commands before the first valid PLACE command will be ignored.

BOMBING

At Random intervals of time the grid boxes will be bomberbarded randomly. The aim is to save our tank from these bomb attacks. Once it falls on the same box where our tank is the tank vaporizes and game gets over.

Game Controls

MOVE

The MOVE command will move the toy drone one unit forward in the direction it is currently facing.

LEFT

The LEFT command will rotate the drone 90 degrees to the left (counter-clockwise) without changing the position of the drone.

RIGHT

The RIGHT command will rotate the drone 90 degrees to the right (clockwise) without changing the position of the drone.

REPORT

The REPORT command will announce the X,Y position and the facing direction (F) of the drone. This can be in any form.

ATTACK

The ATTACK command will cause the drone to fire a projectile 2 units ahead of its current position and explode on the surface.

If there are not 2 free spaces on the surface in the direction that the drone is facing, the ATTACK command should be ignored.
